Welcome to **CapybaraUI** — a modern, elegant, and developer-focused component library for building production-ready interfaces **with power and precision**.  
Built on top of **Tailwind CSS** and **Radix UI**, it blends **beautiful design**, **accessibility**, and **developer experience** into a seamless workflow.

Whether you’re prototyping a new app or shipping to production, **Harbor UI** gives you everything you need to build fast — and look good doing it.

---

## <ShieldCheck className="inline size-5 text-primary" /> Why CapybaraUI?

CapybaraUI is designed for **speed, consistency, and scalability**.  
No more design struggles or boilerplate chaos — just clean, reusable, and accessible components that _work_.

